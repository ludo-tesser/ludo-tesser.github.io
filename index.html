About me

I did my PhD in Janine Splettstoesser's group at Chalmers University of Technology in Gothenburg, Sweden, where I studied theoretical quantum transport and quantum thermodynamics, with a lot of focus on out-of-equilibrium fluctuations.

Before my PhD, I studied Physics at University of Pisa and Scuola Normale Superiore.
