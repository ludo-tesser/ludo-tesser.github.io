About me

I am currently a PhD student in Janine Splettstoesser's group at Chalmers University of Technology in Gothenburg, Sweden. My research focuses on theoretical quantum transport, fluctuations, and nonequilibrium thermodynamics in electronic nanosystems, but I am also interested in quantum thermodynamics and stochastic thermodynamics.
